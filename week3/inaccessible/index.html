What is a Flarmlurv?
The Flarmlurv is a legendary creature that has been spotted roaming the streets of Stockholm since the early 1800s. This elusive animal is known for its shimmering blue-green fur, six legs, and its distinctive humming sound that locals describe as "melodic yet unsettling."
