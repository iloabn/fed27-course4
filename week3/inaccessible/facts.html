Everything About the Flarmlurv
Physical Characteristics
The Flarmlurv possesses a unique physiology that sets it apart from any known animal species. Its most distinctive feature is its shimmering blue-green fur that appears to change color depending on the angle of light.
Six articulated legs with padded feet for silent movement
Large, luminescent eyes adapted for night vision
Retractable antennae used for echolocation
Weight: approximately 8-12 kg
Lifespan: Unknown (estimated 50+ years)
Habitat & Behavior
Flarmlurv are exclusively found in Stockholm, Sweden. They prefer urban environments with access to water, making the archipelago and waterfront areas their primary habitat. They are strictly nocturnal and extremely shy around humans.

Frequently Asked Questions
Is the Flarmlurv dangerous?
No documented cases of Flarmlurv aggression towards humans exist. They are extremely timid and will flee at the first sign of human presence.
Where is the best place to spot a Flarmlurv?
The waterfront areas of Djurgarden and Gamla Stan have the highest concentration of sightings, particularly during late autumn months.
How can I help protect the Flarmlurv?
Support local conservation efforts, report sightings responsibly, and never attempt to capture or disturb a Flarmlurv if you encounter one.
